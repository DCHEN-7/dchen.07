Excel Revenue Forecast

What can we expect of our revenue next year?


Objective: Provide a simple forecasting revenue model which will allow us to identify the current trajectory of our revenue.

Key Takeaways:

Revenue Seasonality Graph
Based on the collected data, it is clear that we have a seasonality effect within our revenue time series; and therefore, a seasonal forecast may be beneficial in generating insight into this dataset.


Model Forecasting
Based on our Excel forecast, we should be expecting $36,459,960.09 in 2019, $46,586,656.57 in 2020, and $51,853,831.14 in 2021. Of course, this dataset is from before the pandemic, so we'd now know this information would likely be false. Additionally, the limitations of this dataset meant that our model could only take into account the first of each year. For my next Excel forecasting project, I would definitely investigate into more comprehensive datasets, ones in which provide time series data at set consecutive intervals (e.g. every 30 days) in order for a stronger forecast.
